Title:
Margaret I. Saunders Correspondence, March 1945
Creator:
Saunders, Margaret I. (Margaret Isabelle Henderson), 1914-1999.
Date Created:
1945-03
Description:
V-Mail correspondence by Margaret I. Saunders to her husband Lee Saunders, March, 1945. V-Mail for March 13, 1945, does not have Part 8. Lee Saunders served in the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers in the segregated, all African-American unit of Company C, 1332nd Engineer General Service Regiment. He would serve in the Pacific Theater of World War II in New Guinea, the East Indies, and Papua New Guinea. Margaret Saunders describes her and her family's experiences at home near the end of WWII.
