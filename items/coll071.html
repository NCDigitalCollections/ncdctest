Title:
Commission. Publicity. Clippings, articles, etc., January 1964-January 1965
Creator:
North Carolina. Governor's Commission on the Status of Women.
Date Created:
1963; 1964; 1965
Description:
Newspaper clippings on the activities of the Commission on the Status of Women and the accomplishments of its members. The questionnaire used to gather statistics for the official report is in the "Women Workers in North Carolina" article published in the Tar Heel Woman.
