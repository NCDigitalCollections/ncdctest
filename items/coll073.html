Description:
Newsletter "Chatter Box" for New Homemakers of America from 1945 to 1965. Future Homemakers of America collection, 1929-1984, includes correspondence, reports, news clippings, lists of officers, audits, programs, budgets, photographs, and other records on the history of Future Homemakers of America, New Homemakers of America, and Home Economics Related Occupations. New Homemakers of America, a club for African Americans, merged with Future Homemakers of America when school segregation ended.
